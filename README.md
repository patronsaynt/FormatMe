A simple, intuitive desktop resume builder and editor. Create, customize, and export professional resumes in minutes.

## Features

Choose from professional resume templates (Classic, Modern, TwoColumn) to fit the needs of your work opportunities

Edit all sections of your resume with a real-time preview, including:
  - Personal information
  - Work experience
  - Education
  - Certifications
  - Custom sections
  - Formatting options

**Import Existing Resumes** — Parse and import resume data to jumpstart your editing, or save your progress and pickup later

🔄 **Drag & Drop** — Reorder sections and items effortlessly to organize your resume your way

🚀 **Cross-Platform** — Built with Tauri for fast, lightweight performance on macOS, Windows, and Linux

## Installation
Grab the the installer for your OS under **Releases**.

## Usage

1. **Open FormatMe** — Launch the application
2. **Select a Template** — Choose your preferred resume template
3. **Fill in Your Information** — Add your personal details, work history, education, and more
4. **Customize** — Use the live preview to adjust formatting and styling
5. **Export** — Save as PDF or DOCX when you're ready to share
